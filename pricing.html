Frequently Asked Questions

How is the 3-Technician / 1,000 Endpoint limit enforced?
It isn't. We operate on a strict honor system. SDA contains zero Digital Rights Management (DRM), zero "phone-home" telemetry, and zero background licensing checks. We built this platform for raw speed and instant live-call resolution. We refuse to introduce artificial latency or network overhead just to verify a license tier. If your environment exceeds the Community limits, we trust that your enterprise procurement and compliance teams will purchase the required Professional seats.
How does the platform scale to 20,000 endpoints?
Because SDA is 100% agentless, it doesn't rely on thousands of persistent, resource-heavy endpoint connections. The platform leverages highly optimized PowerShell Runspace Pools and aggressive multi-threading to keep your asset maps lightning-fast at scale. This allows a single centralized installation to manage up to 20,000 endpoints natively, without requiring complex SQL databases or distributed scanning nodes.
Does the Free Tier lack any features?
No. We believe powerful IT orchestration should not be locked behind a paywall. The Community Free Tier includes 100% of the SDA capabilities, including the Interactive Training Engine and the Theme Manager. The only difference is the legal licensing threshold for your organization's size and access to priority vendor support.
